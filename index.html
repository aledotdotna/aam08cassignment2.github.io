Fitness First Bugis

Located in the bustling shopping mall in Singapore's Central Business district, Fitness First Bugis calls Bugis Junction its home. Amongst some of your favourite boutiques and brands, Fitness First brings excerise and keeping an active lifestyle easier and more accessible. With emphasis on convenience and quality, FF Bugis provides members with access to quality equipment, opulent amenities and well-trained trainers, to equip them with the best experience. FF Bugis even has a steam room for members to recover after a workout.

On a 15,995 square feet plot, with 5 meters high ceiling, FF Bugis is the prime workout location, suited for both autonomous training or workouts tailored to your needs by a personal trainer. With 7 workout zones, and the largest Group Exercise studio, FF members enjoy the privilage engaging in almost any form of strength and condition exercises they wish. Some examples would be Animal flow and HIIT exercises. Moreover, FF Bugis offers Zumba and yoga classes, to offer a more dynamic workout from the traditional weights training.

Fitness First Bugis is an accessible location - located above Bugis MRT and along streets with several bus services. Bugis Junction is not only connected by the East-West and Downtown MRT lines, but also convenient from several busstops along Victoria Street and North Bridge Road.

Come down to FF Bugis for a quick workout and pamper!
